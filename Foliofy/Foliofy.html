FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

What is a portfolio tracker?

A portfolio tracker helps you monitor and manage your investments in real time, providing insights into performance and growth.

How do I connect my wallet?

Simply link your wallet address through our secure platform to start tracking your assets across blockchains.

What assets can I track?

You can track cryptocurrencies, NFTs, and investments across multiple blockchains and layers.

Is the platform free to use?

We offer free and premium plans to suit your needs. Explore features and upgrade as you grow.

How does it work?

Our platform uses blockchain’s open ledger to securely access real-time data from decentralized networks. We transform this raw data into a user-friendly format, offering clear insights into your portfolio’s performance and key metrics, helping you make informed, data-driven decisions.
